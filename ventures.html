Developh Innovation

Students are in the best space to become a founder: they're in the prime time for learning, failing, and building. Some of the world's largest and most successful companies were started by students. There's no better time for students to build, fail, and change the world. Let's bring this potential to Southeast Asia.

Before anything: we invest in you.
Whether you're envisioning a campaign or a startup — whatever stage your project is, we'll work to make it happen and learn. Our Innovation team has collaborated with leading mentors, diverse spaces, investors, and advocacy groups to help put your ideas into reality.


Remote Incubation

Incubatees are welcomed in cohorts and go through our remote incubators. Participants are matched with mentors, given grant funds (no strings attached), and go through sessions structured for their needs with international speakers. Incubatees are guaranteed support from the Developh Innovation Lab.


Innovation Lab

Developh nurtures students and youth as product and project managers, product designers, engineers, and more to work with our student teams. These students provide development and growth assistance to our incubatees and in-house projects, coupled with support from international mentors in different technology and technology-adjacent industries.

If you're in need of team members or guidance in the technical development of your project, our Innovation Lab is here to help you.
