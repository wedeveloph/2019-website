We're living in an age where the youth can change the world from their bedrooms.

Yet in the Philippines, only 13% of households have access to a computer, minorities in tech still face discrimination, and countless parts of the world lack computer science education. Tech may be everywhere, but only for a small part of the population.

We're creating beyond Silicon Valley—from the parts of the world most in need.
We aim to connect the youth to creation, because their presence in the tech pipeline early on sets them to be creative, career-ready, and the founders we need today. When the technology we have today doesn't reflect the youth from all around, there's an issue. In a world fueled by technology, every student deserves to be a part of coding the future to truly live in a world built by all, for all.

We build tech for social good, for purpose, and with passion—operating across the globe, led by students teaching, building, pitching, solving, and spreading creation.

Community

We believe in diverse communities and spaces that encourage students to connect, learn, and support one another. Our chapters program connects students on-ground, and we run one of the largest open tech communities.

Education

Through our chapters and online work, we run a student-created computer science and innovation curriculum called DevEd, host workshops and training programs, and educate students on both critical technical and soft skills.

Opportunity

We elevate tech-minded students with career and skill enrichment programs. We believe in equitable access to funding and spotlight platforms, delegations to events and conferences, and internship/career guidance.

About Developh

To date, Developh has impacted over 60,000 students through our initiatives on accessible creation—principled towards making the tech pipeline more reflective of the youth in the spaces we come from. We uplift and reach out to students, devise a creative open-source computer science/innovation curriculum launched through our chapters and workshops, and run a fellowship and other accelerator perks for students to pursue their ventures.
