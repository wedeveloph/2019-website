ABOUT US

We're living in an age where the youth can change the world from their bedrooms.

By making entry paths to technology more accessible, Developh invests in student founders to diversify the innovation we see.

In its most successful spaces, technology is incredibly student-driven—but this isn't the case in developing nations.

Developh is an international nonprofit investing in student-led innovation for social good. We aim to make technology more equitable and reshift the technology pipeline: engaging students in startup ecosystems across the Asia-Pacific and giving students the critical skills, support, and community to succeed during the best time for them to be a founder.
By helping more young people explore and pursue pathways in technology, we're shaping an ecosystem more representative of all.

Since 2016, we've run education programs to teach code, design, and entrepreneurship to underserved communities and built our online community. In 2019, we're launching Collab: by-students for-students accelerator focusing on impactful collaborations with mission-based groups; and Technology.ph, a youth-driven digital publication on everything tech.

In a world fueled by technology, every student deserves to be a part of coding the future.
